# Quick External Validation (15 min)

Complete these steps to claim the final Growth point (10/10) and reach 100/100.

## 1. Social Preview (2 channels, ~5 min)

Share a RunIt URL in two places and confirm the OG card renders (image, title, description).

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Post `https://runit.dev` or `https://github.com/buildingopen/runit` on X/Twitter |
| 2 | Post the same URL on LinkedIn or Slack |
| 3 | Confirm the preview card shows: title "RunIt - AI writes code. RunIt makes it real.", description, and the social image |

**Evidence:** Screenshot or note the two channels used. Update `docs/evidence/phase3/2026-03-13-external-validation.md`.

## 2. Launch Copy Feedback (3 readers, ~5 min)

Send `docs/LAUNCH_KIT.md` (or a short summary) to 3 people. Ask: "Is this clear? How would you position RunIt in one sentence?"

**Evidence:** One-line feedback from each. Update the evidence file.

## 3. First-App Onboarding (2 users, ~5 min)

Ask 2 people to follow `docs/LAUNCH_FIRST_APP.md` (or the web flow at runit.dev). Note any friction.

**Evidence:** "Completed" or friction points. Update the evidence file.

## After Completing

1. Fill in `docs/evidence/phase3/2026-03-13-external-validation.md` (create from template below if needed).
2. Update `docs/PRR_SCORECARD.md` Growth row to `10/10` and total to `100/100`.
